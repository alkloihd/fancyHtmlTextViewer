🎭 MIRROR MAGIC 🎭

Body Movement Storytelling Game

🌟 Welcome to Mirror Magic! 🌟

Get ready to move, mirror, and make magic with your friends! This special game helps you tell stories with your whole body. You'll copy your friend's movements and create amazing characters together!

🎯 How to Play

🏁 Getting Ready ▼
  • Find a Partner: Choose a friend to be your mirror buddy
  • Make Space: Stand facing each other with arms stretched out
  • Pick Cards: Put all character cards face down in a pile
  • Get Your Score Sheet: Each pair gets one score sheet
🎮 Playing the Game ▼
👥 Round 1: Leader and Mirror
One person leads, the other mirrors for 30 seconds, then switch!
✨ Round 2: Together Magic
Both pick characters and create a story scene together!
